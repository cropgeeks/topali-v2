Reordering Sequences

The display order of sequences can be reordered to aid visual navigation of an alignment file.

To do this, first select the sequences you want to move. These sequences can then be moved up or down the display area by selecting Alignment | Move Sequences Up or Alignment | Move Sequences Down from the menu bar. A third option, Alignment | Move Sequences to Top will move the selected sequences to the top of the grouping.

Note than in addition to visually reordering the sequences, their new positions within the alignment will be reflected in any output files that are exported from the project, such as saving the alignment under a new name or in a different alignment format.
