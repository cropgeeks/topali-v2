TOPALi v2.5

Welcome to TOPALi v2.5, developed by Bioinformatics & Statistics Scotland to provide a range of high-performance, phylogenetic methods for analysing DNA multiple alignments.

To get started quickly with TOPALi, please consult the QuickStart Guide which leads you through a brief tutorial about using the application. You should also consult the TOPALi Overview which gives a brief outline of the application and how it works.

More detailed help information is provided by this online help system, and most buttons and controls within TOPALi also provide popup tooltip information that descibe their purpose. Additional support can be provided by sending an email to topali (at) bioss.ac.uk

If you use TOPALi in your work, we ask that you cite the following paper as a reference:

  • Milne I, Wright F, Rowe G, Marshal DF, Husmeier D and McGuire G (2004), TOPALi: Software for Automatic Identification of Recombinant Sequences within DNA Multiple Alignments, Bioinformatics 20 (11), 1806-1807

Any other comments, queries or bug reports can also be directed to topali (at) bioss.ac.uk
